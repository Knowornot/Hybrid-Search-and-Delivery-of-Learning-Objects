Course website with syllabus, problems, course notes and presentation material. The course covers rudiments of computer programming and some of the essentials of the C programming language. Students taking this course are not expected to have any previous experience with computer programming.

This is a fast-paced course, and at times the amount of material covered may seem unreasonable, especially for novice programmers.  The instructor encourages the students to ask questions, and get things understood then and there during the class.
