The graduate program in aerospace engineering at Georgia Tech has the following areas of specialty:

    * Aerodynamics and Fluid Mechanics (AFM)
    * Aeroelasticity and Structural Dynamics (ASD)
    * Flight Mechanics and Controls (FMC)
    * Propulsion and Combustion (PC)
    * Systems Design and Optimization (SDO)
    * Structural Mechanics & Materials Behavior (SMM).
