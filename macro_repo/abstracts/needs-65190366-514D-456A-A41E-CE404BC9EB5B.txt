The five-year BS/MS program leads to both the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Architectural Engineering in five years. This program is open to exceptional students who are admitted to the graduate program at the end of their junior year. Students applying for this program must have a minimum grade point average of 3.0, and score more than 1000 on the Graduate Record Examination. The course requirements for the five-year BS/MS program are shown on the website.
