Type:  Institutional Transformation.
Year Awarded: 2008. 

Description: The Northeastern University project includes a well justified set of activities and interventions to achieve transformation. Highlights of this include the activities to deal with potential cultural differences between immigrant and native faculty of both sexes, as well as a focus on encouraging international research collaborations for faculty. This will be an important contribution to the understanding of institutional transformation in a global context.
