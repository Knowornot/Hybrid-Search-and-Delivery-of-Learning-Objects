"The Department of Computer Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison is consistently ranked as one of the top ten computer science departments in the country. Our faculty, together with our staff and graduate students, have established a tremendous learning environment for graduate studies in computer science.

In particular, the Department has internationally recognized research programs in Artificial Intelligence (primarily Computer Vision and Machine Learning), Computational Biology, Computer Architecture and VLSI, Computer Graphics, Computer Networks, Computer Security, Database Systems, Numerical Analysis, Operating Systems, Optimization, Performance Analysis, Programming Languages and Compilers, and Theoretical Computer Science.  Extramural research funding exceeds $7 million annually."
