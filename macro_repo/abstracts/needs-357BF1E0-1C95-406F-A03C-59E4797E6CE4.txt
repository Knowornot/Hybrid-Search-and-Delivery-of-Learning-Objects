Best practices from the NSF ADVANCE Program to increase the representation and advancement of women in academic science and engineering careers.

In order to measure change before and after a program has been initiated, assessment must be considered and data collected. Various types of surveys and measures of change are listed below in the following categories.
* Assessment Plan

* Climate Survey
* Faculty Survey

* NSF Required Data Indicators

* Recommendations And Best Practices

* Statistics

* Status of Women Reports.
