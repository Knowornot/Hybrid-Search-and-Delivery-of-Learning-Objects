The EECS Department currently offers MS, MSE, and Ph.D. degrees in the following three programs (through the Rackham Graduate School):

(1) Computer Science & Engineering
(Hardware, Intelligent Systems, Software, Theory, VLSI).

(2) Electrical Engineering 
(Applied Electromagnetics and RF Circuits, Circuits & Microsystems, Optics and Photonics, Solid State, VLSI).

(3) Electrical Engineering: Systems
(Control, Communications, Signal Processing). The EE pgram has the following possible areas of concentration:
(1) Circuits and Microsystems, (2) Applied Electromagnetics and RF Circuits, (3)Optics and Photonics, Optics and Photonics and VLSI."
