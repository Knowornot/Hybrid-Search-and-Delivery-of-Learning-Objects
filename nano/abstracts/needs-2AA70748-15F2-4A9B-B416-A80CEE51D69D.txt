This web-based software teaches the user concepts about nanotechnology and nanocomputers. A nanometer is a billion of a meter and spans approximately 10 atomic diamters.  Nanotechnology is the technology for designing and applying &quot;nanosystems.&quot; The software is broken down in the following sections:
- Tutorial Introduction
- Guide to Selected Introductory Information from the Web
- Tutorial Introduction
- What are Nanotechnology &amp; Nanocomputers?
- Proposed Technologies for Future Nanocomputers    The software features information on the following topics:
- Overview of Nanoelectronics 
- Overview of Quantum Dots 
- Overview of Molecular-Scale Electronics
- Feature Article on Nanoelectronics from High Technology Careers Magazine
- Overview of Nanoelectronics on Texas Instruments Corp
