"Nanotechnology Industries was established in 1998 to distribute information and provide resources about nanotechnology and related emerging technologies that will transform our world in every imaginable way.
To encourage the broadest possible awareness , foster the pursuit of knowledge, communication and innovation. To provide a common forum, sustain and support technological growth, and be a source of direction.
Geared towards promoting the research development and application of nanotechnology, work includes, although not exclusively, web design, collective data, online public awareness, electronic news service, consultation, leads to request for information, collaboration work and the publication of relevant material. Nanotechnology Industries maintains two email lists and has previously published two issues of the Nanotechnology Industries Newsletter, which are still available."
