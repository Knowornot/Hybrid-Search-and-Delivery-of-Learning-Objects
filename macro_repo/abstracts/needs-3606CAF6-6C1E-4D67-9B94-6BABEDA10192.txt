E77 is an introductory course on computer programming for lower-division students in 
science and engineering. The principal goal of the course is to teach basic computer pro- 
gramming concepts and apply them to computer-based problem-solving methods. The 
course stresses hands-on computer programming using MATLAB, a powerful high-level 
programming environment. Website contains lecture slides and Matlab exercises. Course is taught at the University of California at Berkeley.
