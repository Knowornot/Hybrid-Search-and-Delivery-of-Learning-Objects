Report of the pilot coalition program to conerve the Brazilian rain forest. The International Advisory Group of the Pilot Program, composed of 14 international and Brazilian experts, meets twice a year to review progress of the Program and to make recommendations on Program strategy and projects.

During its twelfth meeting in July 1999, the International Advisory Group (IAG) made a special contribution to the rethinking of the Program. The site has links to the main chapters of the IAG's 12th report.
