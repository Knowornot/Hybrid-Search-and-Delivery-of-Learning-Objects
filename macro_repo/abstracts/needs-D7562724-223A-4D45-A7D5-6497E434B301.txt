A Software Engineering concetration can be obtained from the Graduate Program in Computer Science and Engineering which offers a degree in the following Major Areas of Concentration:
(1) hardware systems, (2) intelligent systems, (3) software systems, (4) Theory of computation, and (5) VLSI and CAD. Topics under software systems include: "Collaborative computing, compiler design, parallel & concurrent programming, distributed systems, fault-tolerant computing, file systems, mobile computing, information retrieval & database systems, data mining, Web-based data management, Internet protocols & architectures, network security, operating system & architecture interactions, and real-time computing."
