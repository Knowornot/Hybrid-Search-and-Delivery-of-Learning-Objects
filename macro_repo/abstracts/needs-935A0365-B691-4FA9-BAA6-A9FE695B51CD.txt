Best practices from the NSF ADVANCE Program to increase the representation and advancement of women in academic science and engineering careers.  Understanding how to encourage and propagate women in science and engineering many programs, studies, and initiatives must take place. The following categories explore how to best support faculty development in academia. Topics include:

* Awards

* Bias

* Career Opportunities

* Climate and Culture

* Funding and Grants

* Leadership Development

* Mentoring

* Negotiation

* Networking

* Pipeline

* Professional Development
* Promotion

* Recruitment
* Interviewees

* Search Committees
* Retention

* Teaching

* Tenure.
