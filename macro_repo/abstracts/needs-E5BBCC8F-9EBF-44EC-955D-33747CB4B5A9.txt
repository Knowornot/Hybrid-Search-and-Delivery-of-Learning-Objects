"The Department of Petroleum Engineering offers both master's and Ph.D. degrees as described in the links below. The degree programs are designed to produce graduates with a high level of competence in the broad field of petroleum engineering. The curriculum covers the essential areas of drilling, production, and reservoir engineering. Graduate research is conducted in reservoir simulation, well test analysis, reservoir characterization, multiphase flow in pipes, mechanics of tubulars, cuttings transport, formation damage, artificial life, separation technology, and improved oil recovery."
