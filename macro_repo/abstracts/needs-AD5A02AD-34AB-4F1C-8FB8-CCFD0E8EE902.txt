"For purposes of administering the department doctoral program, research is considered to be carried out in six Graduate Areas:
I. 	  Systems, Communication, Control and Signal Processing
II. 	  Computer Science
        III. 	  Electronics, Computers and Systems
IV. 	  Energy and Electromagnetic Systems
V. 	  Materials and Devices
VII. 	  Bioelectrical Engineering.


In addition, research opportunities and programs are available in the Life Sciences in Computer Science."
