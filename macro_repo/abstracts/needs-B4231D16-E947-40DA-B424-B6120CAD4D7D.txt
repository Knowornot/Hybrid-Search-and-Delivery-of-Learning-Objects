Goals: designed to guide and support disadvantage students at critical junctures from a GED through the completion of a post-secondary degree. The IMSE program begins with multiple proven bridge and support activities provided by FOCUS: HOPE including remedial math and reading program, an industry-certified training program in network administration, network installation, and desktop and server administration; professional devlopement workshops; a student load program; a community food bank; and an on-site child-care program. The second phase of the IMSE program combines a BA in Information Systems and Technology offered by WSU with a coordinated support progrm. The cooordinated support plan at WSU includes tutoring and e-mentoring programs, culturally sensitive advising, emerging scholars sections of introductory programming courses, social networking and participation in industry internships and academic research.
