"The Manufacturing Education Program (MEP) is part of the educational mission of the Manufacturing Research Center (MARC) at the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech).

Intently focusing on enhancing the knowledge of engineering graduate students in manufacturing research and technology, the MEP program gives students the critical, hands-on experience necessary to succeed in a highly competitive, multidisciplinary environment.

The MEP is accomplishing its educational objectives through three program offerings:

    * The Manufacturing Certificate
    * The International Option
    * GM Manufacturing Scholarships.
