"Graduate study in Computer Science is a full-time program. Students are admitted only with the Ph.D. as the degree objective. Students join a research group and participate in research from the very beginning, so it is desirable that applicants have a defined area of interest before applying. The first year emphasizes course work and master's thesis research. Students in their second year complete their master's thesis prior to the candidacy examination.
