Type:  Institutional Transformation;
Year Awarded: 2008. 

The LEADER Consortium: Launching Equity Across the Dayton Entrepreneurial Region.

Description: The project at Wright State University is a collaborative effort within the Dayton, Ohio region between Wright State (lead, public doctoral), Central State University (HBCU, public comprehensive), University of Dayton (private Catholic), and the Air Force Institute of Technology (Federal graduate institution), and will impact 450 STEM faculty. This partnership provides an opportunity to leverage each institution's strengths and close geographic proximity in order to achieve transformation at each institution.
