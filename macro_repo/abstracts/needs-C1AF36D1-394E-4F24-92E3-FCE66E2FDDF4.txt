The Earthquake Hazards Program (EHP) of the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) is part of the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction Program (NEHRP) lead by the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA). The page connects the user to information on monitored national and regional seismic activity in the United States by the USGS, and internationally monitored activity in cooperation with the Incorporated Research Institutes for Seismology (IRIS).
